The Stars Above is a Terraria expansion mod that adds a wide variety of content and a new important game mechanic:
the Starfarer system. With over 1 million downloads in less than a year, The Stars Above guarantees a unique experience for any playthrough.

Choose between either the Umbral or Astral Aspect, and unlock unique equippable abilities
within the Stellar Array, mixing and matching them to perfect your build.

With the latest update, explore pre-designed dungeons seperate from the original world through the magic of Cosmic Voyages, meeting new allies and challenging new foes. Bring your friends along too!

Converse with your Starfarer with hundreds of unique dialogue lines- you will become stronger side-by-side with them,
and arm yourselves for the challenges to come.

Unlock FIVE incredibly powerful Stellar Novas to turn the tide of battle; Stellar Novas have unique sub-abilities exclusive to each Starfarer, so find one that works for you! By defeating bosses, Stellar Novas will become stronger!

Wield over 60 incredibly diverse weapons, with charged attacks, alternate abilities, and malleable damage types.

[h1]STARFARERS[/h1]

The Stars Above's flagship feature: Meet Asphodene and Eridani, masters of Astral and Umbral respectively.
Empowered by their strength and led by their guidance, you will be able to challenge any foe Terraria has to offer. These charismatic deuteragonists boast over 2000+ lines of unique dialogue for bosses, biomes, and more.

[h1]WEAPONS[/h1]

Obtain extraordinary weapons with layers of strategy and myriad powers to overwhelm your foes. Weapons are incredibly unique and can be adjusted to any damage class- including features such as charged attacks, timed counters, energy gauges, and more. Brandish beloved weapons from your favorite game franchises re-imagined into the world of Terraria.

[h1]STELLAR ARRAY[/h1]

Master the Stellar Array, a powerful supplement to player growth.
With 24 powerful passive abilities separated into 3 distinct tiers, you can mix and match to become the strongest Terrarian- buildcraft and skill expression tailored to any playthrough.

[h1]COSMIC VOYAGES[/h1]

Explore the Moirae Galaxy through a brand-new system added in The Stars Above: Celestial Cartography. With the help of Subworld Library, obtain rare materials, powerful accessories, and more. (Alternate material sources are provided in Multiplayer!)

[h1]ADDITIONAL CONTENT / MOD CROSSOVER CONTENT[/h1]

The features of The Stars Above doesn't stop here; express yourself with unique pets or empower yourself with the mod's unique accessories.

Turn Aspected weapons to Rogue Damage from Calamity, or Symphonic/Radiant/Throwing Damage from Thorium!

Your Starfarers will have unique dialogue for content from the following mods:
Calamity, Secrets of the Shadows, Verdant, Fargo's Souls, the Spirit Mod and the Thorium Mod. More will come soon!
Additionally, some separate scaling is present for the Calamity Mod. (This can be disabled in the Mod Settings.)
